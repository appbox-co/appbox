# Launch Week is Coming

We've been building. A lot.

If you've been following along over the past year, you've seen the pieces coming together. The new network with our own AS number and 100G AMS-IX connectivity. The infrastructure overhaul. The website rebuild. All of that was laying the groundwork for what's coming next.

Starting **Monday, March 9th**, we're running our first ever Appbox Launch Week. Five days. Five launches. Something new dropping every single day, Monday through Friday.

## What to Expect

I'm not going to spoil what's coming. Where's the fun in that?

What I will say is this: we've been working on some of these features for over a year. Some of them are things you've been asking for since day one. Some of them are things you didn't even know you wanted yet. And one of them is, honestly, something I never thought we'd be able to pull off.

Every day next week, we'll publish a blog post and send out an email with that day's launch. Each one is a proper feature release, not some minor tweak we're dressing up to fill a slot. These are real, substantial updates that change what Appbox can do.

Five days. Five reveals. That's all I'm giving you for now.

## How to Follow Along

There are a few ways to make sure you don't miss anything:

- **Email**: we'll send you an email each morning with the day's announcement. You'll receive these automatically if you have an Appbox account or are registered on our [billing system](https://billing.appbox.co/).
- **Blog**: every launch gets a full blog post with all the details, right here on [appbox.co/blog](https://www.appbox.co/blog). You can also subscribe via [RSS](https://www.appbox.co/feed/blog.xml) to get updates in your reader of choice.
- **GitHub Discussions**: join the conversation on [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/appbox-co/appbox/discussions) as each feature drops. We'd genuinely love to hear what you think.

## Why Launch Week?

Honestly, we thought about doing one big announcement email. But there's just too much to cover. Each of these features is big enough that it deserves its own proper introduction, its own blog post, and its own moment for you to take it in.

## One More Thing

I've been doing this for a long time now, and I can honestly say I've never been this excited about a week of releases. The team has put in an incredible amount of work, and I think you're going to love what's coming.

See you Monday.
