# We Did It - Our Network is Live!

Remember back in April when we announced we were building our own network infrastructure? Well, I'm thrilled to tell you that as of yesterday, it's finally live.

This has been months in the making, and honestly, seeing it all come together has been one of the most satisfying moments since we started Appbox. Let me tell you what's changed.

## Enterprise-Grade Hardware

We've deployed Nokia 7750 SR-1 routers across our infrastructure. If you're not familiar with Nokia's routing gear, these are the same routers that power some of the largest ISPs and data centers in the world. They're built for reliability and performance at scale, and they're now handling all of your traffic.

This isn't consumer or prosumer equipment. This is proper enterprise networking hardware that gives us the control and capabilities we need to deliver the performance you deserve.

## Our Own IP Space

We're now routing traffic through our own IP addresses under our Autonomous System ([AS209870](https://apps.db.ripe.net/db-web-ui/lookup?source=RIPE&type=aut-num&key=AS209870)). This means we have complete control over how your data travels across the internet.

Why does this matter? Because we can optimize routing specifically for Appbox users, implement better DDoS protection, and in the future, offer features like dedicated IP addresses for those who need them.

## 100G AMS-IX Connectivity

The big one. We're now peering with AMS-IX at 100 gigabits per second. AMS-IX is one of the largest internet exchanges in the world, and having direct access means your traffic takes the fastest possible route to major networks and content providers.

In real terms, this means:
- **Lower latency** - your connections are faster and more responsive
- **Better speeds** - more bandwidth means less congestion
- **Improved reliability** - direct peering means fewer points of failure

We've been testing this over the past few weeks with a subset of traffic, and the results have been exactly what we hoped for. Now everyone gets to benefit.
