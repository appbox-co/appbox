## Cookies Policy

This section explains the cookies that we use on our services to ensure a consistent and effective user experience and a secure environment.

### Compliance with UK Privacy Regulations

Our cookies policy complies with the UK Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) and the UK GDPR. In accordance with these regulations:

1. We obtain your consent before placing non-essential advertising, profiling, conversion attribution, or product analytics cookies on your device
2. We provide clear and comprehensive information about each cookie or similar storage technology we use
3. We make it easy for you to withdraw, object to, or change your choices at any time
4. We do not make access to our website conditional on consenting to non-essential cookies

We may use limited statistical analytics without prior opt-in where this is permitted by PECR. This analytics mode is used only to understand aggregate website and service performance, such as page views, paths, referring domains, device/browser types, loading performance, and similar traffic statistics. It is configured separately from advertising and conversion tracking, does not enable session recording, does not create advertising audiences, and is not used to profile individual visitors. You can object to this analytics through our cookie preferences.

Advertising pixels, remarketing, persistent marketing attribution, and linking a visitor's journey to purchases or conversions are only used where the relevant consent has been given.

We use both session and persistent cookies:

- **Session cookies** remain in your browser during your browser session only, i.e., until you leave the website.
- **Persistent cookies** last beyond the current session and are used for many purposes, such as recognizing you as an existing user, and will remain valid until their set expiry date, unless deleted by the user before the expiry date.

These cookies are further classified into the following categories:

- **Strictly Necessary & Security**
  - These cookies are essential for websites on our platforms to perform their basic functions and should not be disabled. We also use them to make your interactions with our Services faster, more reliable, and to prevent attacks such as cross-site scripting.
- **Performance & Analytics**
  - These cookies and similar technologies allow us to count visitors and traffic sources to evaluate and enhance our site's performance. In statistical mode, we use them only for aggregate service improvement. With your consent, we may also use product analytics to understand individual journeys through our website and services.
- **Targeting**
  - These cookies and similar technologies are used to measure advertising, attribute conversions, and deliver advertisements more relevant to you and your interests. We only use these where you have given the relevant consent.
- **Functionality**
  - These cookies help the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization.

### Strictly Necessary & Security Cookies

- `PHPSESSID`
  - Cookie generated by applications based on the PHP language. This is a general purpose identifier used to maintain user session variables. It is normally a random generated number, how it is used can be specific to the site, but a good example is maintaining a logged-in status for a user between pages.
- `psp_session`
  - This cookie is used to maintain an anonymized user session by the server on our status page.
- `_GRECAPTCHA`
  - Google reCAPTCHA sets a necessary cookie (\_GRECAPTCHA) when executed for the purpose of providing its risk analysis.
- `authorization_token`
  - When logged into the dashboard, we store a JWT (JSON Web Token) for authentication purposes. This token is essential for maintaining a secure, authenticated session.

### Performance & Analytics Cookies

- `_ga`, `_ga_LXBTWJ4Y7B`
  - These cookies are associated with Google Analytics. They help distinguish unique users by assigning a randomly generated number as a client identifier. They are included in each page request in a site and used to calculate visitor, session, and campaign data for the site's analytics reports.
- `ph_phc_*_posthog`
  - Used by PostHog for website and product analytics. Our statistical analytics configuration uses memory-only pageview tracking for aggregate service improvement. Persistent PostHog storage, product journey analysis, and person-level analytics are used only where the relevant consent has been given.
- `mtc_id`
  - This cookie is used to track user behavior and interactions to enhance the website's performance and user experience.

### Targeting Cookies

- `mautic_focus_1`
  - This cookie is used by the Mautic Marketing Automation software to track visitor engagement and interaction with website pop-up content and forms to improve user experience and website effectiveness.
- Google Ads, Microsoft Advertising, and Reddit Ads tags
  - Used for advertising measurement, conversion attribution, and remarketing where the relevant consent has been given.
- `appbox.marketing_attribution`
  - Browser storage used to preserve advertising click IDs and campaign parameters between the Appbox website and billing checkout where advertising attribution consent has been given.

### Functionality Cookies

- `mautic_device_id`
  - This cookie is used to identify the visitor's device and behavior. It tracks user interactions and preferences on the site to enhance user experience and for tracking purposes.

### Additional Cookies

- A random string of characters
  - Used on our status page.
- `__cookie_consent`
  - Used by our status system for cookie consent management.
- `WHMCS*`
  - Used by our billing system for session management.
